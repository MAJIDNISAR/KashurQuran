Our Vission & Plan! In Sha ALLAH

Audio

First step in our endevour is to basically create audio availability, make audio of the translation available for the kashmiri speaking population.
The major challenge we are facing is to break the audio of kashmiri translation into ayah basis, which is a huge task for 6,236 ayah of Glorious Quran.
We would require volunteers in the endevour with audio tools and high speed internet connections.

+

API

Here we are going to develop Nodejs, a robust restful API, with proper documentation, for later stage for web and mobile platforms.
We would require help here financially to put up a nodejs server for the amount of traffic for creating a Web /Mobile readers and audio players.
We would also implore help here from volunteers for kashmir digital script and text of translation of the Glorious Quran.

+

Mobile

We require volunteers for Mobile platform be Android and IOS development once our API is matured.
we would want apps to be able to do both readers as well as audio functionality. Readers would be used to read the script of Glorious Quran and its Kashmir script Verbatem.
We would also implore mobile developers to help us here as well, be it IOS or Android, to use the latest architectural patters on development and develop world class product.

=

Kashur Quran

The ultimate fruition of the effort we would put in, will be we shall have on digital map of the world, where people could come to read and listen to Glorious Quran, we will have an API, which developers could use for further reseach and development.
We would also have apps on across all mobile platforms.
Let us all come together in our humble endevour, and hold on to rope of the ALLAH.
